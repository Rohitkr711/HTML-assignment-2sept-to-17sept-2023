Q). Define accessibility in the context of web development. Discuss why it is essential to create accessible websites and how it benefits
    different user group?
Ans: Accessibility in web development refers to the practice of designing and developing websites that can be used by people of all 
abilities. It involves creating websites that are easy to navigate, read, and use for people with disabilities, such as visual impairments,
hearing impairments, or motor disabilities. Making a website accessible benefits different user groups, including people with disabilities,
older users, and those with temporary or situational disabilities. It also improves the overall user experience, as accessible websites can
be easier to use and more intuitive for all users.
