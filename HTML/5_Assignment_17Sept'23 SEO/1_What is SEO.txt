Q). What is SEO and Explain the importance of SEO?
Ans:  SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, it is a process through which we can optimize a website's content and structure 
      to rank it higher in search engine results pages (SERPs) and increase organic traffic on search engines like Google, Yahoo,
      Bing etc.
      Importance of SEO - 
      SEO is important because, it helps businesses to reach their target audience, improve their online visibility, and drive more 
      conversions. By optimizing their website for search engines, businesses can increase their chances of being found by potential
      customers who are searching for their products or services online.
